PMS Comparison for AI-First Co-Hosting
An evaluation of the top short-term rental property management systems, scored against our specific stack: n8n automation → Claude API → Google Sheets → Slack approval → Softr owner portal. The right PMS is the foundation that everything else sits on.

What we're optimizing for

Our stack is non-standard. We don't use the PMS's built-in messaging or automation — we route everything through n8n + Claude API. That means our evaluation criteria are different from a typical co-host. We care about:

  • API & webhook quality — Rich webhook payloads (ideally full entity inline), clean REST API, minimal extra calls needed
  • Cost at scale — Per-property pricing as we grow from 5 → 20 → 50+ properties across multiple owner-clients
  • Owner accounting & statements — Built-in trust accounting, fee splits, and exportable owner reports (feeds our Claude-generated monthly narratives)
  • Channel management reliability — Solid Airbnb + VRBO + Booking.com sync with no double-booking risk
  • No vendor lock-in for AI — We bring our own AI (Claude via Anthropic API). We don't want to pay for the PMS's built-in AI that we won't use
  • Setup speed vs. long-term ROI — Can we get a new owner-client live quickly while maintaining automation quality?

The Verdict
Ranked for Our Use Case
⭐ Best Fit — Primary PMS

1. OwnerRez

Remains our recommended PMS, and the research reinforces that decision. The full-entity webhook payload (Aug 2025+) is a massive advantage — it means our n8n workflows receive everything in a single event with zero follow-up API calls. That simplicity compounds across all four workflows.

Owner accounting is the best in the category, which directly powers our Claude-generated monthly narrative reports. The sliding-scale pricing makes it the most cost-effective option as we scale past 10+ properties. The one limitation — messaging API requires a partnership agreement — is a known tradeoff we've already documented a workaround for (route via email or platform native messaging).

API: 9.5/10 Cost at Scale: 9/10 Owner Acctg: 10/10 AI Compat: 10/10

2. Hospitable

Solid runner-up and our documented "fast alternative" for a reason. Fastest setup time, clean v2 API, and the included dynamic pricing saves on PriceLabs costs. The main concern is their aggressive AI feature push — their auto-reply system can conflict with our Claude messaging pipeline if not carefully disabled. Also, pricing gets surprisingly expensive at scale: 25 properties on Mogul runs ~$430/mo vs OwnerRez's ~$300.

Best for: onboarding a client who's already on Hospitable, or when a new client needs to be live within 48 hours.

API: 8.5/10 Cost at Scale: 6.5/10 Owner Acctg: 8/10 AI Compat: 7/10

3. Hostaway

A strong all-in-one platform with 300+ integrations and the broadest channel distribution. The Open API is capable, and the first PMS to hit $1B valuation (Feb 2026) signals long-term viability. However, opaque pricing (requires a sales call, contract, and setup fee) is a dealbreaker for our model where we're onboarding owner-clients who each need their own accounts. Dynamic pricing is an extra per-listing cost on top of an already higher base price.

Best for: mid-to-large management companies (20+ properties) who want everything under one roof and don't mind paying premium pricing for it.

API: 7.5/10 Cost at Scale: 5/10 Owner Acctg: 7/10 AI Compat: 7/10

4. Guesty

The enterprise gold standard with the most mature API and the widest channel distribution (60+ direct integrations). If we were managing 100+ properties with a team of 5, Guesty would be the pick. But for our lean AI-first model, it's overkill. The pricing is opaque and expensive, accounting is an $8/listing/mo add-on, analytics another $5/listing/mo, and setup takes 2–4 weeks. We'd be paying for enterprise infrastructure we don't need because our automation layer replaces most of it.

Best for: large property management companies (50+ units) with dedicated ops teams.

API: 9/10 Cost at Scale: 3/10 Owner Acctg: 6/10 AI Compat: 6/10

5. Lodgify

Lodgify has the best direct booking website builder in the market and fair pricing for small operators. But the lack of an open API is a fundamental disqualifier for our stack. Without webhook support and programmatic access, we can't connect it to n8n, can't trigger Claude workflows, and can't automate anything. It's a manual-operation PMS, not an automation-first one. The 1.9% booking fee on the Starter plan also erodes margins.

Best for: self-managing hosts who want a beautiful direct booking site and don't need external automation.

API: 2/10 Cost at Scale: 7/10 Owner Acctg: 3/10 AI Compat: 9/10 (no conflict)

Final Recommendation

Primary PMS: OwnerRez. No change from our current playbook. The full-entity webhook payload, best-in-class owner accounting, transparent sliding-scale pricing, and zero AI conflicts make it the clear winner for an AI-first co-hosting operation. The 60–90 minute setup per property is an investment that pays off immediately in automation quality.

Secondary / Fast-Deploy: Hospitable. Keep this as the documented alternative for clients who are already on it or need to be live within 48 hours. Disable their native AI automation on day one to avoid conflicts with our Claude pipeline.

Not recommended for our stack: Hostaway, Guesty, Lodgify. Hostaway and Guesty are strong products but priced for larger operations that need their all-in-one feature set — features our AI layer already replaces at a fraction of the cost. Lodgify's lack of API makes it incompatible with any automation-first approach.
