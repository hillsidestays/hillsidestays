Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Dylan — a Western North Carolina local, builder, and operator. I’ve called WNC home for the past 10 years after growing up in Winston-Salem and graduating from Appalachian State University with a BSBA focused on Digital Marketing and Sales. My career has largely been in early-stage and high-growth environments where I’ve worked across sales, marketing, and operations.

That path eventually led me into the short-term rental space, where I worked in national sales operations helping investors buy and sell STR properties across the country. Along the way I developed a strong interest in the systems behind successful rental businesses — from listing optimization and SEO to automation and scalable workflows.

Over time I realized something: most STR investors weren’t lacking opportunity — they were lacking infrastructure.

So I built Hillside Stays to solve that problem.

Today I help owners across Western North Carolina and the Smoky Mountains turn great homes into high-performing rentals through strong listings, smart pricing, direct booking infrastructure, and streamlined operations.
