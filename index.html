Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Dylan.
WNC local. Builder. Operator.

I've called Western North Carolina home for the past 10 years. I grew up in Winston-Salem, NC and graduated from Appalachian State University with my BSBA — concentration in Digital Marketing with a minor in Sales. Since then, I've been drawn to early-growth and startup environments where I could take on outsized roles across sales, marketing, and operations.

That path led me into the short-term rental space, where I worked in national sales operations and helped investors acquire and sell STR properties. Over time, I've become an expert in sales, systems architecture, and short-term rental operations — and I saw a massive opportunity to bring all of those skills together under one roof.

So I left my job and went for it. Hillside Stays is my mission: a value-focused business that provides the full stack of what STR investors need — systems architecture, backend operations, SEO, direct bookings, workflow automation, and applied AI.

I also work with independent hosts and smaller managers who want to level up their operations. Three ways we can work together:

  • Hire me — I build and run the entire system for you
  • Learn from me — I teach you and give you the resources to do it yourself
  • Work with me — We build it together, side by side

When I'm not building systems or optimizing listings, you'll find me out in the mountains that made me fall in love with this place. I hike, climb, kayak, and stay involved with the Nature Conservancy and local community events. I care deeply about WNC and its people — and that's exactly why I built this business here.
